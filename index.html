Relay Availability

The Refactor Realm Relay operates on a hybrid model designed for reliability and sustainability.

Daily Online Hours

10:00 AM – 10:00 PM Eastern Time
(With natural day-to-day variation.)

Off-Hours Behavior

Outside the daily window, the relay enters Gateway Mode:

  • The public endpoint stays online
  • Billing and Stripe webhooks remain active
  • A friendly offline message is shown
  • No writes are accepted during this period

Why This Model?

This approach keeps costs low, ensures predictable uptime during peak Nostr activity, and provides a stable endpoint 24/7. As the subscriber base grows, the relay will transition to a full 24/7 environment.
